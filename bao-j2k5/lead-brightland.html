Brightland

Modern pantry brand with Kitchen Cabinet subscription buried under PDP copy and Whole Foods shoppers never rerouted online.

Revenue est. $20M Shopify Plus Priority: Medium

Why This Matters Now

Brightland’s seasonal oils and Whole Foods placements are driving first-time traffic, but the subscription CTA sits below recipe copy. Shoppers can’t see the value or delivery details before they bounce back to search.

Killer Insight

The Kitchen Cabinet PDP has never run a hero vs. subscription-first test—pricing and delivery details sit 1200px down the page, so ad traffic never sees the offer.

What’s happening

  • PDP hero is a story block; subscription CTA and value prop are buried beneath long-form copy.
  • No visual explanation of what’s inside the subscription or how deliveries work.
  • Mobile layout pushes reviews + recipes ahead of pricing, so the add-to-cart is off-screen.

What to fix

  • Reorder the hero so subscription price, cadence, and CTA appear before any narrative block.
  • Insert a two-row “What you get” visual grid plus delivery timeline immediately under the hero.
  • Keep the buy box + subscription toggle sticky on mobile, sitting above recipes and reviews.
