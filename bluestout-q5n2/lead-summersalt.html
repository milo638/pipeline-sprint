Summersalt

Swim + lifestyle brand layering “Everywhere” apparel onto core swim without matching navigation or lifecycle.

Revenue est. mid-8 figures Shopify Plus Priority: High

Why This Matters Now

Summersalt is spending on spring/summer swim and “Everywhere” apparel at the same time, yet the site and lifecycle still highlight only one story, so paid traffic dies right before peak demand.

Killer Insight

Capsule launches recycle the exact swim PDP—there’s never been a test where the hero, copy, or CTA swaps to match the new drop, so paid traffic hits outdated proof.

What’s happening

  • Swim + apparel nav/PDP share one template, so old drops stay live.
  • Checkout remains USD-first; EU/UK shoppers see surprise totals.
  • Klaviyo drops highlight only the latest story; older capsules never resurface.

What to fix

  • Add drop chips (Swim / Everywhere / etc.) in the hero so image, badges, and the CTA rewrite instantly when tapped.
  • Compress the carousel into a 3-up gallery and pin a sticky CTA + fit picker to the bottom edge on mobile.
  • Insert a color/size availability strip directly under price with live inventory + fit notes to kill reload friction.
