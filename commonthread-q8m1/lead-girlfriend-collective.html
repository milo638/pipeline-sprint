Girlfriend Collective

Recycled-fabric activewear brand running Liteweight, warehouse sales, and resale without dedicated UX or conversion path paths.

Revenue est. $35M Shopify Plus Priority: Medium

Why This Matters Now

Girlfriend Collective’s Liteweight relaunch is live, but PDPs and post-click journey still look like the Compressive core line, so launch spend isn’t converting or retaining.

Killer Insight

The brand sells nuanced fabrics, yet digitally it treats every shopper—and every fabric—the same.

What’s happening

  • Liteweight PDPs reuse Compressive copy/imagery.
  • ReGirlfriend resale traffic exits with no owned follow-up.
  • Warehouse-sale and full-price entrants share identical post-click journey.

What to fix

  • Swap PDP modules based on fabric + persona.
  • Tag sale vs. resale vs. full-price cohorts inside the post-click data layer.
  • Pipe ReGirlfriend events back into upgrade/rebuy journeys.
