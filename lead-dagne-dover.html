Revenue est. $45M Shopify Plus Priority: High

Why This Matters Now

Dagne Dover is leaning into baby and travel campaigns, but PDPs and flows still mash caregivers and commuters together, wasting spend before peak summer travel.

Killer Insight

The brand sells organization, yet its digital experience treats every shopper exactly the same.

What’s happening

  • Work, baby, and travel gear share the same nav/PDP stack.
  • Influencer bundle pages autoplay video and take 6–7s on mobile.
  • Klaviyo welcome/post-purchase flows stay one-size-fits-all.

What to fix

  • Swap PDP + bundle modules per persona.
  • Rebuild influencer landing system for fast mobile loads.
  • Route Klaviyo flows by persona tags from quiz/checkout.
